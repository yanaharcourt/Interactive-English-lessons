üìç Word Order

Where do we put frequency adverbs?

‚úÖ Before Main Verb

  • I always study
  • She often runs
  • They sometimes cook

‚úÖ After "to be"

  • I am always happy
  • She is usually busy
  • They are never late

‚úÖ After Auxiliary

  • I have never seen
  • She has always lived
  • They will often visit

üåü Examples with different sentence types:

Simple Present: I always eat breakfast.
With "to be": She is sometimes tired.
Present Perfect: We have never been there.
