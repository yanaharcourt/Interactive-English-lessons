Les Dizaines

Tens from 20 to 60

Round Tens

These are straightforward - just memorise them!

Tens 20-60

  • 20 - vingt (van)
  • 30 - trente (tront)
  • 40 - quarante (karont)
  • 50 - cinquante (sank-ont)
  • 60 - soixante (swas-sont)

How to form 21-29, 31-39, etc.

  • 21 = vingt-et-un (twenty and one)
  • 22 = vingt-deux (twenty-two)
  • 35 = trente-cinq (thirty-five)
  • 48 = quarante-huit (forty-eight)
  • 59 = cinquante-neuf (fifty-nine)

üìù Pattern

For 21, 31, 41, 51, 61 use ET (and)
vingt-et-un, trente-et-un, quarante-et-un

For all other numbers: just use a hyphen -
vingt-deux, trente-trois, quarante-cinq

Nombres 70-79

The Tricky Seventies!

Important!

French has NO separate word for 70!

70-79 = soixante (60) + 10-19

The Pattern

60 + numbers 10-19

soixante-dix = sixty-ten

70 = soixante-dix
71 = soixante-et-onze
75 = soixante-quinze

Why?

Historical Reason

Base-20 counting system

Old French used base-20
(like "four score" in English)
This system remains today

Examples 70-79

  • 70 = soixante-dix (60+10)
  • 71 = soixante-et-onze (60+11)
  • 72 = soixante-douze (60+12)
  • 75 = soixante-quinze (60+15)
  • 79 = soixante-dix-neuf (60+19)
