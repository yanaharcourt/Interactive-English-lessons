Possessives vs Articles

Adjectifs Possessifs vs Articles

Don't Confuse Them!

Possessive adjectives replace articles - never use both!

With Possessives

  • Mon chat est mignon - My cat is cute

  • Sa maison est grande - His/her house is big

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Error
