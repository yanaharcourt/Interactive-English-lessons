O'clock

Exact Hours

What is O'clock?

Use O'CLOCK when the time is EXACTLY on the hour

No minutes - just the hour (1:00, 2:00, 3:00...)

Morning Hours (AM)

From midnight to noon

  • 1:00 - It's one o'clock
  • 3:00 - It's three o'clock
  • 6:00 - It's six o'clock
  • 9:00 - It's nine o'clock
  • 12:00 - It's twelve o'clock (noon)

Afternoon/Evening Hours (PM)

From noon to midnight

  • 13:00 - It's one o'clock (PM)
  • 15:00 - It's three o'clock (PM)
  • 18:00 - It's six o'clock (PM)
  • 21:00 - It's nine o'clock (PM)
  • 00:00 - It's twelve o'clock (midnight)
What time is it? - It's five o'clock.
The meeting starts at ten o'clock.
I wake up at seven o'clock every day.

Half Past

30 Minutes Past the Hour

What is Half Past?

HALF PAST = 30 minutes after the hour

When the minute hand points to 6 (bottom of the clock)

British English Way

Half past + hour

  • 1:30 - Half past one
  • 3:30 - Half past three
  • 6:30 - Half past six
  • 9:30 - Half past nine
  • 12:30 - Half past twelve

Digital Clock

What you see

  • 01:30 ‚Üí Half past one
  • 04:30 ‚Üí Half past four
  • 07:30 ‚Üí Half past seven
  • 10:30 ‚Üí Half past ten
  • 11:30 ‚Üí Half past eleven
What time is it? - It's half past two.
I have lunch at half past twelve.
The class starts at half past eight.

Quarter Past & Quarter To

15 and 45 Minutes

Understanding Quarters

A quarter = 15 minutes (¬º of an hour)

QUARTER PAST

= 15 minutes AFTER

Moving forward

3:15 = Quarter past three
7:15 = Quarter past seven

QUARTER TO

= 15 minutes BEFORE

Moving towards next hour

3:45 = Quarter to four
7:45 = Quarter to eight

Examples

  • 2:15 = Quarter past two
  • 2:45 = Quarter to three
  • 5:15 = Quarter past five
  • 5:45 = Quarter to six
  • 10:15 = Quarter past ten
  • 10:45 = Quarter to eleven
What time is it? - It's quarter past four.
The movie starts at quarter to seven.

Minutes Past & To

Any Time Between Hours

The Rule

0-30 minutes = PAST | 31-59 minutes = TO

Minutes PAST (0-30)

After the hour

  • 3:05 - Five past three
  • 3:10 - Ten past three
  • 3:20 - Twenty past three
  • 3:25 - Twenty-five past three

Minutes TO (31-59)

Before the next hour

  • 3:35 - Twenty-five to four
  • 3:40 - Twenty to four
  • 3:50 - Ten to four
  • 3:55 - Five to four

üí° Remember!

PAST = We passed that hour (going forward)
TO = Going TO the next hour (approaching)

Ten past five = 5:10
Ten to six = 5:50

AM and PM

Morning vs Afternoon/Evening

AM = Before noon | PM = After noon

AM (Ante Meridiem)

Before midday

Midnight to Noon

12:00 AM - 11:59 AM
Morning time

PM (Post Meridiem)

After midday

Noon to Midnight

12:00 PM - 11:59 PM
Afternoon/Evening/Night

Time Expressions

  • 7:00 AM - Seven o'clock in the morning
  • 12:00 PM - Twelve o'clock (noon/midday)
  • 3:00 PM - Three o'clock in the afternoon
  • 7:00 PM - Seven o'clock in the evening
  • 12:00 AM - Twelve o'clock (midnight)
  • 2:00 AM - Two o'clock in the morning
I wake up at 7 AM (in the morning).
School finishes at 3 PM (in the afternoon).

Daily Routines

Time Expressions for Daily Activities

Learn when to use IN, AT, and ON with time!

AT (specific times)

  • at 7 o'clock
  • at half past three
  • at noon / at midnight
  • at night
  • at the weekend (UK)

IN (parts of day)

  • in the morning
  • in the afternoon
  • in the evening
  • NOT: ~~in the night~~ ‚Üí at night
  • on the weekend (US)
I wake up at 7 o'clock in the morning.
I have lunch at half past twelve.
I do my homework in the afternoon.
I go to bed at 10 PM at night.
