👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Present Simple

Third Person Singular (He, She, It + S)

Today we'll learn why we add -S to verbs when we talk about He, She, It!

🎯 He, She, It

Third Person Singular

He plays, She runs, It works

Always add -S!

👥 I, You, We, They

Other Persons

I play, You run, We work

No -S needed!

📚 Why do we use it?

To talk about habits and facts

Things that happen regularly

Every day actions!

🌟 Quick Example:

I play football → He plays football
We study English → She studies English

⏰
🔄

⏰ What is Present Simple?

Actions we do regularly

Present Simple tells us about:

Things that happen every day, every week, or are always true!

🔄 Habits

  • I brush my teeth every day
  • She goes to school
  • We eat breakfast

Things we do regularly!

💯 Facts

  • The sun rises in the east
  • Water boils at 100°C
  • Cats like milk

Things that are always true!

❤️ Likes & Dislikes

  • I love pizza
  • He hates vegetables
  • They enjoy music

Our feelings and preferences!

🤔

💡 Easy to Remember!

Present Simple = What happens NOW in general
Not just this moment, but in our normal life!

👤 Who are He, She, It?

Third Person Singular

👨 HE (Males)

  • Tom → He
  • My dad → He
  • The teacher → He
  • My brother → He

👩 SHE (Females)

  • Mary → She
  • My mom → She
  • The doctor → She
  • My sister → She

🐱 IT (Things & Animals)

  • The cat → It
  • My phone → It
  • The car → It
  • The weather → It

📝 Important Rule:

ONE person, animal, or thing = He, She, It
When we talk about ONE person (not me, not you), we use He/She/It!
✨
📝

✨ The Magic -S Rule

When do we add -S?

🎯 The Golden Rule:

He, She, It = Add -S
Everyone else = NO -S

🏃‍♂️ Verb: "run"

I run я бегаю
You run ты бегаешь
He runs он бегает
She runs она бегает
It runs оно бегает
We run мы бегаем
They run они бегают

🌟 More Examples:

I eat apples → She eats apples
You play games → He plays games
We watch TV → It works well
🔤
📖

🔤 Special -S Rules

How to add -S correctly

➕ Just add -S

  • play → plays
  • run → runs
  • eat → eats
  • sleep → sleeps

Most verbs!

📝 Add -ES

  • watch → watches
  • wash → washes
  • kiss → kisses
  • fix → fixes

After s, x, ch, sh!

🔄 Y → IES

  • study → studies
  • try → tries
  • cry → cries
  • fly → flies

When Y after consonant!

📝 Special Verbs (Irregular):

have has иметь
do does делать
go goes идти

🧠 Memory Trick:

"He, She, It - don't forget the S!"
Just like a snake makes "Ssssss" sound! 🐍
