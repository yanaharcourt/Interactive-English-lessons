üìê How to Make It

Present Continuous Formula

üéØ The Magic Formula

Subject + BE + VERB + ing

BE = am, is, are

üë§ I am + ing

  • I am reading
  • I am playing
  • I am eating
  • I am sleeping

üë• You/We/They are + ing

  • You are running
  • We are singing
  • They are dancing
  • You are learning

üë®‚Äçüë©‚Äçüëß‚Äçüë¶ He/She/It is + ing

  • He is writing
  • She is cooking
  • It is raining
  • Mom is working

‚ú® Step by Step:

Step 1: Choose the right BE verb (am/is/are)
Step 2: Add -ing to the main verb
Step 3: Put them together!
Example: I + am + play + ing = I am playing!
