📐 How to Make It

Present Continuous Formula

🎯 The Magic Formula

Subject + BE + VERB + ing

BE = am, is, are

👤 I am + ing

  • I am reading
  • I am playing
  • I am eating
  • I am sleeping

👥 You/We/They are + ing

  • You are running
  • We are singing
  • They are dancing
  • You are learning

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 He/She/It is + ing

  • He is writing
  • She is cooking
  • It is raining
  • Mom is working

✨ Step by Step:

Step 1: Choose the right BE verb (am/is/are)
Step 2: Add -ing to the main verb
Step 3: Put them together!
Example: I + am + play + ing = I am playing!

⚖️ Simple vs Continuous

What's the Difference?

The Big Question!

When to use Simple vs When to use Continuous?

Let's find out! 🕵️‍♀️

🔵 Present Simple

Habits & Facts

  • I play football every day
  • She goes to school
  • The sun rises in the east
  • We eat lunch at 12

For:

• Daily habits
• Facts
• Regular actions

🟢 Present Continuous

Now & Temporary

  • I am playing football now
  • She is going to school
  • It is raining today
  • We are eating lunch

For:

• Actions now
• Temporary situations
• Changing things

🎯 Quick Test:

Every day: I play football (Simple)
Right now: I am playing football (Continuous)

Ask yourself: Is it happening NOW or ALWAYS?

🔔 Signal Words

Words that tell us to use Present Continuous

🚨 Time Signal Alert!

These words are like alarms - they tell us to use Present Continuous!

⚡ Right Now Words

  • now - сейчас
  • right now - прямо сейчас
  • at the moment - в данный момент
  • currently - в настоящее время

👀 Look & Listen Words

  • Look! - Смотри!
  • Listen! - Слушай!
  • Watch! - Наблюдай!
  • Be quiet! - Тише!

📅 Today/This Words

  • today - сегодня
  • this week - на этой неделе
  • this month - в этом месяце
  • these days - в эти дни
Look! She is dancing beautifully!
I am studying English this year.
Listen! Someone is singing in the garden.
We are working on a project these days.
